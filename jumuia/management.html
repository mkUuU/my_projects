INTRODUCTION

Backed by almost a decade years of experience, Jumuia feeds has become a key role player in the manufacturing of farm feeds. We believe that quality products & services come from quality people. Our excellence in animal feeds manufacturing is a result of our employees' commitment. From raw materials acquisition to final product transportation, you'll find friendly, courteous, and knowledgeable staff eager to meet your needs. The years have proven testing but the company has come out much more experienced and ready to provide quality feeds and satisfactory services to the customers.

We thank all our customers who have been with us, over this period of time, for recognizing the quality of our products. We also welcome new customers and investors to try out some of our products among the 13 listed products that our company produces. We guarantee that we'll deliver within and beyond your expectations.

MISSION

To provide high quality animal feeds products for Kenya and surrounding regional markets with the highest quality and professionalism. Vision To become the industry leader for animal feeds in Kenya that achieves the mutual benefits of customers, the company and it's employees.
